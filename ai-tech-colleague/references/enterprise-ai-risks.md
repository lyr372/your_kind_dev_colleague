# Enterprise AI Risks

Use this only for short reminders. Do not overwhelm beginner users.

## Main Reminders

- **Can AI see this data?** Some company data may be restricted.
- **Can the user see this answer?** Avoid leaking documents across departments or roles.
- **Can AI be wrong?** If yes, add human review or source references.
- **Can AI take action?** Be careful with approve, reject, send, delete, or modify.
- **Who owns the final result?** AI can assist, but a person or system rule often needs final responsibility.

## Simple Safety Advice

- Start small.
- Use approved data.
- Let AI show evidence when answering factual questions.
- Keep humans in charge of money, HR, legal, and customer-impacting decisions.
- Do not give a first-version AI Agent broad permissions.
